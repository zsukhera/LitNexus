“Night-walking amuses him, then,” I remarked, affecting a careless
manner: in reality as surprised as she was, and anxious to ascertain the truth
of her statement; for to see the master looking glad would not be an every-day
spectacle. I framed an excuse to go in. Heathcliff stood at the open door; he
was pale, and he trembled: yet, certainly, he had a strange joyful glitter in
his eyes, that altered the aspect of his whole face.
