“‘Hindley will be there before me,’ I answered: ‘and
that’s a poor love of yours that cannot bear a shower of snow! We were
left at peace in our beds as long as the summer moon shone, but the moment a
blast of winter returns, you must run for shelter! Heathcliff, if I were you,
I’d go stretch myself over her grave and die like a faithful dog. The
world is surely not worth living in now, is it? You had distinctly impressed on
me the idea that Catherine was the whole joy of your life: I can’t
imagine how you think of surviving her loss.’
