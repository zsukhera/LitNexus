“I shall lift him on to the settle,” I said, “and he may roll
about as he pleases: we can’t stop to watch him. I hope you are
satisfied, Miss Cathy, that you are not the person to benefit him; and
that his condition of health is not occasioned by attachment to you. Now, then,
there he is! Come away: as soon as he knows there is nobody by to care for his
nonsense, he’ll be glad to lie still.”
