“If I were only sure it would kill him,” she interrupted,
“I’d kill myself directly! These three awful nights I’ve
never closed my lids—and oh, I’ve been tormented! I’ve been
haunted, Nelly! But I begin to fancy you don’t like me. How strange! I
thought, though everybody hated and despised each other, they could not avoid
loving me. And they have all turned to enemies in a few hours. They have, I’m positive; the people here . How dreary to meet death,
surrounded by their cold faces! Isabella, terrified and repelled, afraid to
enter the room, it would be so dreadful to watch Catherine go. And Edgar
standing solemnly by to see it over; then offering prayers of thanks to God for
restoring peace to his house, and going back to his books ! What in the
name of all that feels has he to do with books , when I am dying?”
