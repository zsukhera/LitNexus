“Fair means!” she said, in answer to her husband’s look of
angry surprise. “If you have not courage to attack him, make an apology,
or allow yourself to be beaten. It will correct you of feigning more valour
than you possess. No, I’ll swallow the key before you shall get it!
I’m delightfully rewarded for my kindness to each! After constant
indulgence of one’s weak nature, and the other’s bad one, I earn
for thanks two samples of blind ingratitude, stupid to absurdity! Edgar, I was
defending you and yours; and I wish Heathcliff may flog you sick, for daring to
think an evil thought of me!”
