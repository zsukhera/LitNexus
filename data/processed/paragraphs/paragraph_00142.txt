“‘Maister Hindley!’ shouted our chaplain. ‘Maister,
coom hither! Miss Cathy’s riven th’ back off “Th’
Helmet o’ Salvation,” un’ Heathcliff’s pawsed his fit
into t’ first part o’ “T’ Brooad Way to
Destruction!” It’s fair flaysome that ye let ’em go on this
gait. Ech! th’ owd man wad ha’ laced ’em properly—but
he’s goan!’
