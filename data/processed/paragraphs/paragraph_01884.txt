He had his hand in her hair; Hareton attempted to release her locks, entreating
him not to hurt her that once. Heathcliff’s black eyes flashed; he seemed
ready to tear Catherine in pieces, and I was just worked up to risk coming to
the rescue, when of a sudden his fingers relaxed; he shifted his grasp from her
head to her arm, and gazed intently in her face. Then he drew his hand over his
eyes, stood a moment to collect himself apparently, and turning anew to
Catherine, said, with assumed calmness—“You must learn to avoid
putting me in a passion, or I shall really murder you some time! Go with Mrs.
Dean, and keep with her; and confine your insolence to her ears. As to Hareton
Earnshaw, if I see him listen to you, I’ll send him seeking his bread
where he can get it! Your love will make him an outcast and a beggar. Nelly,
take her; and leave me, all of you! Leave me!”
