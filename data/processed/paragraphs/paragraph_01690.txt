“And she looked like it, too! I gave her a little wine. Hareton and
Joseph, who had been wakened by the ringing and the sound of feet, and heard
our talk from outside, now entered. Joseph was fain, I believe, of the
lad’s removal; Hareton seemed a thought bothered: though he was more
taken up with staring at Catherine than thinking of Linton. But the master bid
him get off to bed again: we didn’t want his help. He afterwards made
Joseph remove the body to his chamber, and told me to return to mine, and Mrs.
Heathcliff remained by herself.
