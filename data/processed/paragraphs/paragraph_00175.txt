“Oh, God confound you, Mr. Lockwood! I wish you were at the—”
commenced my host, setting the candle on a chair, because he found it
impossible to hold it steady. “And who showed you up into this
room?” he continued, crushing his nails into his palms, and grinding his
teeth to subdue the maxillary convulsions. “Who was it? I’ve a good
mind to turn them out of the house this moment!”
