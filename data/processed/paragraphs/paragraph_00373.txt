She, supposing Edgar could not see her, snatched the cloth from my hand, and
pinched me, with a prolonged wrench, very spitefully on the arm. I’ve
said I did not love her, and rather relished mortifying her vanity now and
then: besides, she hurt me extremely; so I started up from my knees, and
screamed out, “Oh, Miss, that’s a nasty trick! You have no right to
nip me, and I’m not going to bear it.”
