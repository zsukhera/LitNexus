“I’m afraid, Ellen, you’ll set me down as really wicked; but
you don’t know all, so don’t judge. I wouldn’t have aided or
abetted an attempt on even his life for anything. Wish that he were
dead, I must; and therefore I was fearfully disappointed, and unnerved by
terror for the consequences of my taunting speech, when he flung himself on
Earnshaw’s weapon and wrenched it from his grasp.
