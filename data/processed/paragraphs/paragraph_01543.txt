“Oh!” he sobbed, “I cannot bear it! Catherine, Catherine,
I’m a traitor, too, and I dare not tell you! But leave me, and I shall be
killed! Dear Catherine, my life is in your hands: and you have said you
loved me, and if you did, it wouldn’t harm you. You’ll not go,
then? kind, sweet, good Catherine! And perhaps you will consent—and he’ll let me die with you!”
