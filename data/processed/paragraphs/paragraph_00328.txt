“Oh, such a grand bairn!” she panted out. “The finest lad
that ever breathed! But the doctor says missis must go: he says she’s
been in a consumption these many months. I heard him tell Mr. Hindley: and now
she has nothing to keep her, and she’ll be dead before winter. You must
come home directly. You’re to nurse it, Nelly: to feed it with sugar and
milk, and take care of it day and night. I wish I were you, because it will be
all yours when there is no missis!”
