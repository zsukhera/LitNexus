The work they studied was full of costly pictures; and those and their position
had charm enough to keep them unmoved till Joseph came home. He, poor man, was
perfectly aghast at the spectacle of Catherine seated on the same bench with
Hareton Earnshaw, leaning her hand on his shoulder; and confounded at his
favourite’s endurance of her proximity: it affected him too deeply to
allow an observation on the subject that night. His emotion was only revealed
by the immense sighs he drew, as he solemnly spread his large Bible on the
table, and overlaid it with dirty bank-notes from his pocket-book, the produce
of the day’s transactions. At length he summoned Hareton from his seat.
