“‘Treachery and violence are a just return for treachery and
violence!’ cried Hindley. ‘Mrs. Heathcliff, I’ll ask you to
do nothing; but sit still and be dumb. Tell me now, can you? I’m sure you
would have as much pleasure as I in witnessing the conclusion of the
fiend’s existence; he’ll be your death unless you overreach
him; and he’ll be my ruin. Damn the hellish villain! He knocks at
the door as if he were master here already! Promise to hold your tongue, and
before that clock strikes—it wants three minutes of
one—you’re a free woman!’
