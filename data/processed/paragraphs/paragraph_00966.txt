“I have run the whole way from Wuthering Heights!” she continued,
after a pause; “except where I’ve flown. I couldn’t count the
number of falls I’ve had. Oh, I’m aching all over! Don’t be
alarmed! There shall be an explanation as soon as I can give it; only just have
the goodness to step out and order the carriage to take me on to Gimmerton, and
tell a servant to seek up a few clothes in my wardrobe.”
