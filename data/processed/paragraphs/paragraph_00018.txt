Joseph was an elderly, nay, an old man, very old, perhaps, though hale and
sinewy. “The Lord help us!” he soliloquised in an undertone of
peevish displeasure, while relieving me of my horse: looking, meantime, in my
face so sourly that I charitably conjectured he must have need of divine aid to
digest his dinner, and his pious ejaculation had no reference to my unexpected
advent.
