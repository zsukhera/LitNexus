On the morrow of that Monday, Earnshaw being still unable to follow his
ordinary employments, and therefore remaining about the house, I speedily found
it would be impracticable to retain my charge beside me, as heretofore. She got
downstairs before me, and out into the garden, where she had seen her cousin
performing some easy work; and when I went to bid them come to breakfast, I saw
she had persuaded him to clear a large space of ground from currant and
gooseberry bushes, and they were busy planning together an importation of
plants from the Grange.
