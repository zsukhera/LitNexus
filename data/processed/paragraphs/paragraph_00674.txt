“Have you been listening at the door, Edgar?” asked the mistress,
in a tone particularly calculated to provoke her husband, implying both
carelessness and contempt of his irritation. Heathcliff, who had raised his
eyes at the former speech, gave a sneering laugh at the latter; on purpose, it
seemed, to draw Mr. Linton’s attention to him. He succeeded; but Edgar
did not mean to entertain him with any high flights of passion.
