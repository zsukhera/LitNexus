Oh, how weary I grew. How I writhed, and yawned, and nodded, and revived! How I
pinched and pricked myself, and rubbed my eyes, and stood up, and sat down
again, and nudged Joseph to inform me if he would ever have done. I was
condemned to hear all out: finally, he reached the “ First of the
Seventy-First .” At that crisis, a sudden inspiration descended on me;
I was moved to rise and denounce Jabez Branderham as the sinner of the sin that
no Christian need pardon.
