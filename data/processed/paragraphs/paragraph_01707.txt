“‘Mr. Hareton, and the whole set of you, will be good enough to
understand that I reject any pretence at kindness you have the hypocrisy to
offer! I despise you, and will have nothing to say to any of you! When I would
have given my life for one kind word, even to see one of your faces, you all
kept off. But I won’t complain to you! I’m driven down here by the
cold; not either to amuse you or enjoy your society.’
