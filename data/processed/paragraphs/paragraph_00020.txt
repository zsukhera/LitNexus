Before passing the threshold, I paused to admire a quantity of grotesque
carving lavished over the front, and especially about the principal door; above
which, among a wilderness of crumbling griffins and shameless little boys, I
detected the date “1500,” and the name “Hareton
Earnshaw.” I would have made a few comments, and requested a short
history of the place from the surly owner; but his attitude at the door
appeared to demand my speedy entrance, or complete departure, and I had no
desire to aggravate his impatience previous to inspecting the penetralium.
