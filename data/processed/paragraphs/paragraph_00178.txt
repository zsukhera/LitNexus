“If the little fiend had got in at the window, she probably would have
strangled me!” I returned. “I’m not going to endure the
persecutions of your hospitable ancestors again. Was not the Reverend Jabez
Branderham akin to you on the mother’s side? And that minx, Catherine
Linton, or Earnshaw, or however she was called—she must have been a
changeling—wicked little soul! She told me she had been walking the earth
these twenty years: a just punishment for her mortal transgressions, I’ve
no doubt!”
