“Put that hat on, and home at once,” said I. “I’m
dreadfully grieved at you, Miss Cathy: you’ve done extremely wrong!
It’s no use pouting and crying: that won’t repay the trouble
I’ve had, scouring the country after you. To think how Mr. Linton charged
me to keep you in; and you stealing off so! It shows you are a cunning little
fox, and nobody will put faith in you any more.”
